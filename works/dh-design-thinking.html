D+H Design Thinking

Helping a FinTech giant think about innovation from their customers' point of view.

Home Message Me

Global fintech company Davis + Henderson (D+H) hired our team at Architech to help them reimagine an innovative mobile platform through design thinking. Our small project team (2 developers, 1 designer, 1 strategist, 1 PM) dived right in to understand their business, interview their customers, and build working prototypes to validate our solutions for the next generation of the platform.

We all worked closely with several of D+Hs product managers to research the customer's experience, and then build and validate solutions in order to iterate as quickly possible. In addition to conducting interviews, and contributing to design concepts, I implemented several functional prototypes with Angular.js and a branded version of Google's material design system. Our technical set up enabled me to make changes as quickly as the ideas came out, so that we could, for example, build and deploy UI changes in between usability interviews.
