D+H Design Thinking

Helping a FinTech giant think innovate from their customers' point of view.

Home Message Me

Global fintech company Davis + Henderson (D+H) hired our team at Architech to help them reimagine an innovative mobile platform through design thinking. Our small project team (2 developers, 1 designer, 1 strategist, 1 PM) dived right in to understand their business, interview their customers, and build working prototypes to validate solutions for the next generation of their platform.

We all worked closely with several of D+Hs product managers to research the customer's experience, and then build and validate solutions in order to iterate as quickly possible. In addition to conducting interviews, and contributing to design concepts, I implemented several functional prototypes with Angular.js and a branded version of Google's material design system. Our technical set up enabled me to make changes as quickly as the ideas came out. This lean set up allowed us to do some great things, like building and deploying UI changes in between usability interviews.

Using the Jobs-To-Be-Done methodology, we interviewed several potential users in order to really dig into the customers perspective. This research helped us understand how we could solve real user problems and develop very clear requirements to drive the direction of the platform.

After our initial research stage, we built out and iterated upon a prioritized list of interface components and user flows. We then looped back and tested our original hypotheses through several usability tests and stakeholder interviews. This discovery (and re-discovery) process helped define the capabilities that D+H should invest in, while minimizing the risk involved in launching such a cutting edge initiative.
