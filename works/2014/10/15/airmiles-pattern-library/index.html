Make it easier to do the right things, and harder to do the things that aren't

In an effort to improve collaboration and reduce the time it takes to launch new products, we formed a community of practice with members from several teams and divisions at AIR MILES.

Along with several process improvements, we recently launched version 1 of our pattern library. It consists of documentation that is tied to very modular Sass that can be pulled into any web project via NPM. It is already being used in several environments, and drastically reducing time to build new pages.

Check it out here: pattern-library.airmiles.ca
